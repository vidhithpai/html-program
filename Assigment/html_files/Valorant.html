GAME INFO AGENTS

A 5v5 character-based tactical shooter

Valorant is a free-to-play first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. It was released on June 2, 2020, for Microsoft Windows. The game is a tactical shooter that combines elements of traditional first-person shooters with unique character abilities.

In Valorant, players assume the control of "agents," characters who come from a variety of countries and cultures around the world. Each agent has their own unique abilities that can be used to gain an advantage over the opposing team. The game features a variety of game modes, including a competitive mode where players can rank up and earn rewards.
